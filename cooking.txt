## ajvar
https://www.seriouseats.com/recipes/2013/09/ajvar-serbian-roasted-red-pepper-sauce-recipe.html

5 medium bell peppers
1 medium eggplant
5 tsp minced garlic
1/4c sunflower or olive oil
1 tsp white vinegar
1 tsp kosher salt

be sure to cook the eggplant long enough!

roast peppers on direct flame
roast eggplant w/ indirect heat
de-seed peppers and remove skin
remove eggplant skin
blend with garlic in food processor
add olive oil, vinegar, salt
blend
cook in medium saucepan
bring to simmer on medium heat
drop heat to low for ~30m
